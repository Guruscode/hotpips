I've paid for the mentorship, what's next?+

In this email, you'll find all the access links to the mentorship course and groups: WhatsApp (for three, six and twelve months) or Telegram (for one month). No, it is not. Our mentorship is subscription-based. We offer monthly, three-month, six-month and yearly mentorship subscriptions that can be renewed upon expiration.

Yes, you can. After you have made the payment forward your proof of payment and registered email address to support@firepipsfx.com and the date you want to commence your mentorship.
